There are multiple type of physic laws in Minecraft.
Not every law applies to all objects at all times.
that's why they have been split into three
categories:
<NEWLINE>
§lUniversal Laws§r, applies to everything always.
<NEWLINE>
§lBlock Laws§r, applies to things on the block grid.
<NEWLINE>
§lEntity Laws§r, applies to things on the entity grid.
